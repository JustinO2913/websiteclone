Customer Rights

At TipToe Shoes we are commited to providing our customers with the best shopping experience

To ensure transparentcy and satisfaction, we outline the following rights:

1. Right to fair pricing: Customers have the right to clear and accurate information regarding product description, sizes and availability

2. Right to privacy: Customers have the right to privacy and confidentiality regarding their personal information

3. Right to Non-Discrimination: Customers have the right to be treated with respect and without discrimination

4. Right to Qualitiy: Customers have the right to recieve high qualitiy, durable and highly described products. Each pair of shoes undergoes quality check before being shipped
